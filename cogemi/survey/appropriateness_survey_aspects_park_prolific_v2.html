Social Appropriateness Study

Thank you for taking part. This study investigates how people judge the social appropriateness of everyday actions in public settings.

You will read ~47 short vignettes set in a public park. For each one you will be asked four quick questions: how appropriate you find the action, what you think others would consider appropriate, how common the behaviour is, and how certain you are of your judgement. You will then identify which aspect of the situation most influenced you.

The study takes approximately 20–25 minutes. There are no right or wrong answers.

Your data: Responses are stored securely and linked only to your Prolific ID, which will be removed from the analysis dataset after payment. Participation is voluntary; you may withdraw at any time.
