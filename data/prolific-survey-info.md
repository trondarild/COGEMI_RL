## Study description
*(shown to participants on the Prolific listing before they accept — should describe the task clearly without revealing the hypothesis)*

In this study you will read a series of short descriptions of everyday situations taking place in a public park. For each situation, you will be asked to judge how socially appropriate the action described is, using a simple three-point scale. You will then be asked to identify which aspect of the situation most influenced your judgement.

The study consists of approximately 42 short scenarios and takes around **12–15 minutes** to complete. There are no right or wrong answers — we are interested in your personal judgements.
