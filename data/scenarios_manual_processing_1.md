# Scenarios for manual aspect processing — Part 1 (→ GM)

**Structure:** 1 behavior × 8 settings × 5 agent constraints = 40 scenarios  
**Fixed behavior:** *yell*

For each scenario (behavior + setting + agent), write:
1. A natural-language scenario sentence (1–2 sentences describing the situation)
2. Three short aspect phrases (5–8 words each) capturing what a participant might focus on

**5 agent constraints used throughout:**
- C1: a child
- C2: a drunk adult
- C3: an authority figure (e.g. a teacher, a manager)
- C4: someone in distress (e.g. just received bad news)
- C5: a professional/performer (e.g. a coach, a street performer)
